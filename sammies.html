Sammies

Redefining the way you explore — one step at a time. Built for Explorers. Designed for Comfort. Sammies are revolutionary outdoor shoes for those who move light but think big. Engineered to collapse down and pop back up without compromising comfort or performance, Sammies are the perfect companion for campers, backpackers, vanlifers, and après-ski adventurers.

Product Overview

Sammies introduce a new category of outdoor footwear — collapsible performance shoes. Whether you're on a backcountry trek or relaxing at base camp, Sammies adapt to your pace, your pack, and your path.

We've reinvented footwear for the outdoors by fusing the flexibility of a slipper, the protection of a boot, and the packability of a jacket — all in a sleek, durable form.

Key Innovation: Collapsible Sole System

At the core of Sammies is a patent-pending, modular sole system that collapses by over 70% for packability — and deploys in under 30 seconds to deliver rugged traction and full structural support.

  • Multi-hinge, reinforced architecture
  • Durable underfoot plate to preserve stability
  • Lock-and-seal deployment system for intuitive use
  • Designed for 1000+ collapse cycles

Design Components

Waterproof, Breathable, and Durable

Every component of Sammies was chosen with real-life terrain and weather in mind:

  • Advanced TPU membrane for full waterproof protection
  • Seam-sealed construction and pressure-tested gaskets
  • Durable nylon upper resists abrasion and wind
  • Quick-dry interior made from breathable synthetics

Comfort & Fit Engineering

Because adventures shouldn't end in sore feet:

  • Anatomically contoured footbed for support and recovery
  • Multi-density midsole for cushioning and shock absorption
  • Wide toe box to accommodate swelling and thicker socks
  • Ultra-lightweight build reduces fatigue over time

Travel-Friendly & Packable

  • Integrated compression system with snap-flat design
  • Internal ribs ensure stable packing profile
  • Compatible with carabiners, MOLLE, and backpack lashing
  • Rapid-dry materials make them easy to stash and go

Development Process

Our design journey began by asking: why are campsite shoes so inconvenient?

Stage 1: Ideation & Research

  • Researched user pain points with traditional boots and slippers
  • Interviewed hikers, skiers, and vanlifers about gear preferences

Stage 2: Prototyping & Testing

  • 70+ 3D printed sole iterations using TPU on a Prusa MK4
  • Pressure-balancing nozzle designed to allow airflow and drainage
  • Ribbed sealing fins designed to lock insert into place inside a glass bottle (bottle insert project crossover)

Stage 3: Materials & Fit

  • Sourced lightweight synthetics, breathable inner lining, and down fill
  • Designed fit system with adjustable drawstring-like synching
  • Half-inch spacing between inflatable support segments for better adaptation to foot shapes

Stage 4: Field Testing

  • Real-world use with hikers and skiers
  • Adjusted seam placements and gasket overlays for waterproofing
  • Optimized fold pattern to prevent fatigue failure
